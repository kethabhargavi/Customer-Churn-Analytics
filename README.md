# 📊 Customer Churn Analytics & Business Intelligence using Python

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges for subscription-based businesses such as telecom, SaaS, banking, and internet service providers. Losing existing customers directly impacts revenue and increases customer acquisition costs.

This project performs an **end-to-end Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on customer churn data using Python to identify the major factors influencing customer retention. The analysis transforms raw customer data into meaningful business insights and actionable recommendations that support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

A telecom company wants to understand:

- Why are customers leaving?
- Which customer segments have the highest churn?
- Which customers generate the highest revenue?
- What factors influence customer retention?
- What strategies can reduce customer churn?

This project answers these questions through data analysis rather than predictive machine learning.

---

# 💡 Key Business Insights

Some of the important findings include:

- Customers with **Month-to-Month contracts** exhibit the highest churn.
- Customers with **shorter tenure** are more likely to leave.
- **Higher monthly charges** are associated with increased churn.
- **Fiber Optic customers** contribute higher revenue but also experience higher churn.
- Customers using **Electronic Check** have relatively higher churn rates.
- Long-term customers demonstrate greater loyalty.
- Service-related factors influence churn more than demographic characteristics.

---

# 📌 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following actions are recommended:

- Encourage Month-to-Month customers to switch to long-term contracts.
- Improve onboarding for new customers.
- Introduce loyalty rewards for long-term customers.
- Review pricing strategies for high-risk customer groups.
- Improve customer support for Fiber Optic users.
- Promote automatic payment methods.
- Develop targeted retention campaigns for high-value customers.
